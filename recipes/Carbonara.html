Carbonara recipe

Description

Savor the essence of Rome with our Spaghetti Carbonara. This classic dish boasts al dente spaghetti coated in a luscious blend of eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, crispy guanciale, and a hint of black pepper. A symphony of flavors in every bite, this quick and authentic recipe is your ticket to Italian culinary bliss. Buon appetito!

Ingredients

  • Guanciale
  • Spagehtti
  • Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Pecorino

Method

  1. Cook the spaghetti: Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Pecorino Romano cheese until well combined.
  3. Sauté guanciale: In a skillet over medium heat, sauté the diced guanciale until crispy and golden brown.
  4. Combine and toss: Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with guanciale. Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing quickly to coat the spaghetti evenly.
  5. Adjust seasoning: Sprinkle black pepper to taste and adjust the consistency with reserved pasta water if needed.
  6. Serve immediately: Plate the Carbonara and serve promptly while enjoying the creamy, flavorful goodness. Buon appetito!
